◊ WE ARE ALL IN A WORLD OF HYPNOSIS ◊

   我们都处于世界性的催眠之中。手机推送、网页弹窗、商业广告、宣传标语，甚至各路新闻、社交平台评论、热门的文章和或视频……那些在历史文化中被沿袭的概念，在经济发展中被营造的观念，都成了催眠的介质，并正以无孔不入的方式对我们进行着心理暗示。
   We are all in a world of hypnosis. Mobile push, web pop-ups, commercial advertisements, slogans, even news headlines, comments on social platform, popular articles and videos... Those concepts inherited in history and culture or created in economic development, have become hypnotic mediums and are giving us psychological hints in a pervasive way.


   但更为可怕的是，大多数的催眠披着隐秘的外衣，我们常常被催眠而不自知。而催眠在我们潜意识中植入的错误观念，将极大影响我们对于事物的客观理性判断。
   But what is more terrible is that most hypnosis is cloaked in secrecy, and we are often hypnotized without knowing it. However, the wrong ideas implanted in our subconscious by hypnosis will greatly affect our objective and rational judgment of things.


   我们将常见的催眠手段转换为更直白的视觉表达，从而表现催眠的普遍性与荒谬性，希望能引发对于催眠行为的反思。
   We transform the common hypnotic methods into more direct visual expressions, so as to show the universality and absurdity of hypnosis, and hope to cause reflection of hypnosis.

HYPNOTIC RITUAL

AE 与 PROCESSING 动画

AE & PROCESSING ANIMATION

STEP 1

COMMUNICATION
「解惑」

失眠者蒙眼描绘图腾模样，催眠师通过图腾解读失眠原因，双方由此建立信任。

The INSOMNIAC draw a TOTEM blindfolded. The HYPNOTIST then interprets the causes of insomnia through TOTEM, which builds trust between the two sides.

来源于中国古代巫术中，通过龟甲上的裂纹占卜吉凶的做法。

This comes from the practice of divination through cracks in the tortoise shell in ancient Chinese witchcraft.

STEP 2

INDUCING
「入境」

催眠师睁开眼睛，露出梦境的一角，所有失眠者为之吸引。

The HYPNOTIST opens its eyes and reveals the corner of a dreamland that attracts all INSOMNIAC.

来源于常见的催眠动图，通过图形变化吸引注意力、激发潜意识。

This comes from the common hypnotic gifs, which attract attention and stimulate the subconscious through graphic changes.

STEP 3

VERBAL HINT
「梦呓」

催眠师轻念咒语，在失眠者眼中形成波纹，失去睡眠的人终于进入梦境。

The HYPNOTIST casts a spell that creates ripples in the eyes of the INSOMNIAC, and they eventually drift into dreams.

来源于中国古代的器皿——鱼洗，用双手摩擦两端可让器皿中的水振动，形成波纹。

The reference here is to an ancient Chinese vessel - Yuxi, rub the two ends with the two hands can make the water in the vessel vibration, forming ripples.

HYPNOTIC PROPS

C4D 循环动画

C4D LOOP ANIMATION

 

NO.1

在这个催眠道具中，失眠者在方形滚轮上滚动，不断追赶着上方象征着幸福的宝石体。但宝石体上下浮动，在失眠者即将追赶到时又渐渐升高高。失眠者于是陷入永无止境的翻滚与追赶中。

In this prop, INSOMNIAC will roll on a square rollers, chasing the DIAMOND symbolizing happiness. But the DIAMOND will move up and down, gradually rising as INSOMNIAC is about to catch up. So INSOMNIAC is in a never-ending roll and chase.

“永远在涌现但永远无法实现的新目标”

“new goals that are always being created but never totally achieved”

 

NO.2

这是一枚半红半黑的宝石体，中间插有一面圆形的镜子。宝石体和镜子一同旋转，所以无论从哪个方向观察，都像是一枚完整的黑宝石或者完整的红宝石。 

There is a half black and half red DIAMOND with a round mirror in the middle. The DIAMOND rotates with the mirror so that it looks completely black or completely red in every directions.

“断章取义、以偏概全的言论”

“statements taken out of context”

 

NO.3

失眠者不断跳动、挤压着按钮，上方的宝石体灯也在随之亮起，好像是失眠者控制着灯。但实际上失眠者与灯的亮灭毫无关系，因为按钮并没有在电路中接通，而下方的两小片方体才是控制电路的关建。但失眠者仍在这种催眠中，乐此不疲地上下跳动着。

INSOMNIAC in the center constantly squeeze the button as if it can control the light's on and off. It’s not difficult to find that the two squares below are the critical parts, and INSOMNIAC has nothing to do with the light. But INSOMNIAC is still happily bouncing up and down.

"没有实际功效的心理安慰"

"psychological comfort with no practical effect"

 

NO.4

在这个催眠道具中，宝石体不断左右摇摆，经过中间的圆形玻璃时，会因玻璃的折射而被放大，并在灯光的照射下显得光彩夺目。

In this prop, the DIAMOND keeps swaying from side to side. When passing through the middle round glass, it will be magnified by the refraction of the glass, which makes an ordinary object look dazzling.

"被人为美化但无法兑现的承诺"

"promises that are glorified but can never be fulfilled"

"催眠是人类的一大能力"
"Hypnosis is really a great human ability."
